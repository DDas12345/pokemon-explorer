Battle Mechanics

Mega Evolution

A temporary transformation that increases a Pokémon's power during battle, unlocking new abilities and stats.

Gigantamax

Pokémon grow in size and gain special G-Max moves with unique visual forms and abilities.

Z-Moves

Powerful one-time moves that can turn the tide of battle when used strategically.

Terastallization

A transformation that changes a Pokémon's type, boosting its moves and adding strategic depth.
